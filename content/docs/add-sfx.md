Adding sound effects (SFX) can enhance your stream's TTS experience.

1. Place your `.mp3` or `.wav` files into the designated `SFX` folder.
2. In the JakeyTTS interface, go to the SFX manager.
3. Reload or map the SFX files to specific triggers or commands.
